Open Design by the Numbers
Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — but kept it closed, paid, and cloud-only. Open Design sets that capability free.
Design happens on the desktop. Local files, Figma exports, and code repos are directly readable, and agents get full terminal power — on your own machine.
Open Design plugs in the agents you already have — Claude Code, Codex, Cursor — and every choice becomes design systems, preferences, and memory.
