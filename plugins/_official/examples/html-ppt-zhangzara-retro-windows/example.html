Why Open Design

In April 2026, Claude Design proved for the first time that an LLM can truly design — not write about design, but produce real design artifacts. Yet it is closed-source, paid, and cloud-only. Open Design sets that capability free: frontier AI design, back on every creator's desk.

The Problem

Closed-source and cloud-only means locked to one model, with no agent choice, no self-hosting, and no BYOK. Your files, your repos, and your design taste live on someone else's servers — on someone else's terms.

The Open Answer

Desktop-native, so design happens where your files are. It plugs in the agents you already run — Claude Code, Codex, Cursor — instead of rebuilding them. And it learns your taste: every choice becomes design systems, preferences, and memory.
