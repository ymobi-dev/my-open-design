Chapter 04The Method

The
method.

From idea to delivery in five movements. The agent reads your context; you direct the work — all on your own machine.
01

Start

Describe your goal in one sentence, or pick a starting point from a template or plugin.

02

Direction

Set the visual direction. Palette, type, and spacing flow into generation automatically.

03

Generate

The agent reads full context and produces real runnable files, previewed and edited in a sandbox.

04

Ship

Hand off to engineering, or turn the artifact into a marketing video with HyperFrames.

05

Memory

Every choice becomes context for the next run. The system lands closer to your taste each time.
