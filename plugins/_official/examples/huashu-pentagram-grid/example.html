Section 03 / Why Open Why Open
03
Closed cloud, or open local.
In April 2026, Claude Design proved for the first time that an LLM can truly design — but it is closed-source, paid, and cloud-only: the model is locked, and switching agents, self-hosting, and BYOK are all impossible. Open Design makes that capability open.
