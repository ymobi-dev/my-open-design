// SHT 03 · revision delta

Closed cloud vs Open Design

Claude Design — closed cloud

Locked & metered

  • One model, no agent choice
  • No self-hosting, no BYOK
  • ~$500 a month, cloud-only

Open Design — this drawing

Open & local

  • 21 coding agents, BYOK throughout
  • Runs on your machine, Apache-2.0
  • ~$73 a month — over 80% less
Comparison: the closed cloud incumbent vs Open Design. April 2026: Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — Open Design sets that capability free. Semantic good/bad tokens stay within the locked palette — coral and mint read as redline annotations.
