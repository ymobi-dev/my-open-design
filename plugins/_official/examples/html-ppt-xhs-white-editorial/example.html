How it works
04 / 08

One sentence in, the whole flow out

1
Pick a start: describe your goal in one sentence, or grab a template
2
Lock the visual direction — palette, type, and spacing carry over automatically
3
The agent outputs runnable files, previewed instantly in a sandbox
4
Hand off to engineering or turn into video — choices settle into memory
Every choice becomes next time's context —
it gets you more with every use.

name: my-brand
description: "Single source of truth for brand tokens."

tokens:
  - palette:  "Brand colors stay put — no token drift."
  - type:     "Display / body / mono families."
  - spacing:  "One grid, consistent everywhere."
  - memory:   "Every choice feeds the next generation."
