The Core
02 · The Belief Core

We believe every creator
deserves AI design power.

In April 2026, Claude Design proved for the first time that an LLM can truly design — not write essays, but produce real design work. Yet it is closed-source, paid, and cloud-only: swapping agents, self-hosting, and BYOK are all impossible. Open Design sets that power free.

04/WHY · Belief
Open Design/03 Product Principles
How · Discipline
03 · Product Principles

Three principles turn belief
into product method.

HOW — 1
Desktop-native

Design happens on the desktop: local files, Figma exports, and code repos are directly readable, and agents hold full terminal execution power.

HOW — 2
Integrate, don’t rebuild

Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor on your machine are already strong; Open Design wires them into a complete design workflow.

HOW — 3
Learns you over time

Every choice settles into design systems, preferences, and memory, so the next generation lands closer to what you want.
