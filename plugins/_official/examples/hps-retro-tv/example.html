The insight

Set the capability free

In April 2026, Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — not write about design, but produce real design artifacts. Yet it shipped closed.

  • Closed-source, paid, and cloud-only — locked to one model
  • No agent choice, no self-hosting, no BYOK
  • Your files, repos, and Figma exports live on the desktop
  • The agents on your machine are already powerful enough

The gap, in one line

Frontier AI design, back on every creator's desk — open-source, running locally, wired into the coding agents you already use.
