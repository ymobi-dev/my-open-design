Findings · detail Open DesignPhase III
Notice · 05
Why we exist

In April 2026, Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — and then locked that capability in a closed, paid, cloud-only box.

What changed

An LLM can truly design — not write about design, but produce real design artifacts, end to end.

  • Real artifacts — prototypes, web pages, slides, and HTML video, not pictures of them.
  • Full workflow — from idea to delivery, in one continuous loop.
  • Proven in April 2026 — Claude Design showed it works at production quality.
The moment LLM design became real

Why it's stuck

Closed-source · paid · cloud-only.

It is locked to one model, with no agent choice, no self-hosting, and no BYOK. Your files and your keys live on someone else's machine.

The cost is not just the monthly bill — it is every workflow you cannot own, version, fork, or run on your own terms.

No agent choice · no self-hosting · no BYOK

What Open Design does

  • Runs locally — your data stays in a local .od/ SQLite store; API keys never leave your machine.
  • Plugs in 21 coding agents — Claude Code, Codex, Cursor and more, auto-detected from PATH.
  • Stays open — Apache-2.0; fork, self-host, and commercial use all allowed.
