Why Open Design

SET
DESIGN
FREE.

In April 2026, Claude Design proved for the first time that an LLM can truly design — not write about design, but produce real design artifacts.

Yet it is closed-source, paid, and cloud-only: locked to one model, with no agent choice, no self-hosting, no BYOK.

Open Design sets that capability free — frontier AI design, back on every creator's desk.
