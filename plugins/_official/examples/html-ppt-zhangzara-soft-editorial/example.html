Foreword
ii

In April 2026, Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design.

Not write about design — produce real design artifacts: prototypes, pages, slides, and video, straight from a prompt.

Yet that capability shipped closed-source, paid, and cloud-only: locked to one model, with no agent choice, no self-hosting, no BYOK. The frontier arrived, then closed its doors.

Open Design sets that capability free. This deck is the short version — what it is, what it makes, and how to bring it onto your own machine.

Core capabilities

Everything runs on your own machine — your files, your agents, your keys — and every choice you make compounds into design memory.

On your desktop

Desktop-first by design — local files, Figma exports, and code repos are readable in place, and agents get full terminal power.

  • 21 coding agents — auto-detected from PATH, zero config.
  • Full-stack BYOK — Anthropic / OpenAI / Azure / Google plus 14 media providers.
  • HTML video pipeline — programmatic video via HyperFrames.
Apache-2.0 — fork, self-host, commercial use all allowed.
