The insight

AI design got locked up

In April 2026, Claude Design proved an LLM can truly design — real design artifacts, not essays about design. Then it locked the door.

  • Closed-source and paid, running only in the cloud
  • Locked to one model — no agent choice, no BYOK
  • No self-hosting; your work never lives on your machine
  • The coding agents already on your desk sit unused

The answer, in one line

Frontier AI design, back on every creator's desk — local-first, agent-driven, and Apache-2.0, on the machine you already own.
