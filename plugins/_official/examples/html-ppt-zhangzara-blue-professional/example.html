Why Open Design

Positioning

Open, local, and agent-native by design

  • In April 2026, Claude Design proved for the first time that an LLM can truly design — producing real design artifacts, not writing about design.
  • Yet it is closed-source, paid, and cloud-only: locked to one model, with no agent choice, no self-hosting, and no BYOK.
  • Open Design is desktop-native: local files, Figma exports, and code repos are directly readable, and agents get full terminal power.
  • It plugs in the agents already on your machine — Claude Code, Codex, Cursor — instead of rebuilding them.
  • It learns your taste: every choice becomes design systems, preferences, and memory, so the next generation lands closer to what you want.

Process

Pick a Starting Point
Describe your goal in one sentence, or start from a template or plugin.

Set the Visual Direction
